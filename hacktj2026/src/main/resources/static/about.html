About This Project

Learn who made this and how Terac was used

Who Made This

This webpage and program were created by Tiya Gezie, Sohini Ray, & Elisha Kim. Our goal was to design a tool that helps high school students manage cognitive overload and mental stress by analyzing their daily schedules.

We recognized that heavy schedules, especially for students at TJ, can lead to burnout and cognitive overload. That’s why we aimed to create a program that rebalances an individual’s weekly calendar based on their burnout risk, tasks, and cognitive load.

How We Used Terac

Terac was used to enhance the functionality of this project by connecting user schedule information to experts in four fields: sleep science, mental health, learning, and student wellness. These experts analyze the data to provide professional recommendations for rebalancing cognitive load. With Terac, we were able to:

  • Obtain schedule information directly from the user’s Google Calendar
  • Securely and anonymously process personal data
  • Provide expert-backed recommendations for managing mental stress

By combining Terac with standard web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), we created a seamless and informative experience to help students manage their schedules and mental well-being effectively.
